AegisAI is an AI-powered platform built specifically for UK financial services firms, designed to make FCA compliance and financial decision-making faster, simpler, and more accessible. At its core, it combines two intelligent tools into a single, easy-to-use interface that anyone in a financial services business can sit down and use without needing a legal or accounting background.

The first tool, AegisAI Comply, acts as an always-available compliance expert. Firms can paste in any document — a financial promotion, a client letter, a suitability report, or a set of terms and conditions — and within seconds receive a detailed analysis identifying potential FCA rule breaches, referencing the specific sections of the FCA Handbook that apply, rating the severity of each issue, and providing clear, plain-English recommendations for fixing them. It covers the full range of regulatory frameworks including COBS, MCOB, CONC, Consumer Duty, and SM&CR.

The second tool, AegisAI CFO, serves as an on-demand financial intelligence assistant for UK business owners and firm principals. Users can describe their financial situation — whether that's a cash flow problem, a hiring decision, a question about what to do with surplus capital, or a late-paying client — and receive honest, structured analysis grounded in the UK business context, including VAT, Corporation Tax, and PAYE considerations.

Together, the two tools give smaller financial services firms access to the kind of expert guidance that was previously only available by paying expensive consultants or compliance officers, available instantly, around the clock, at a fraction of the cost.
